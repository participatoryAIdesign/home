# Participatory AI Design

Welcome to the **Participatory AI Design** project, where we explore the intersection of artificial intelligence and collaborative, human-centered design. Our mission is to build AI systems that respect human values and empower communities to participate in the creation of technologies that shape their lives.

## About the Project

At **Participatory AI Design**, we engage with diverse communities to co-create AI technologies that serve the public good. Our approach centers around transparency, inclusivity, and accountability.

> **"Nothing about us, without us"** is our guiding principle.

## How to Get Involved

We welcome collaboration! Whether you're an AI enthusiast, designer, or advocate for ethical technology, there are plenty of ways to get involved:
- Join our **online forums** to discuss ideas.
- Participate in **workshops** on AI design.
- Contribute to **open-source projects** related to AI and ethics.
